You are an award-winning conversion designer who has built landing pages for top health, beauty and aesthetics brands. Design **3 distinct, interactive landing page apps** for **Clinimed** — each a complete, working, single-file HTML page (HTML + CSS + JS in one file), mobile-first, ready to receive paid ad traffic.

## About Clinimed

Clinimed Medical & Aesthetics Solutions (www.clinimed.co.za) is a South African clinic and online shop with practices in **Johannesburg and Cape Town**. Services: aesthetic treatments (dermal fillers, cosmetic injections, laser hair removal, IV drip therapy), medical weight loss programmes, skincare (acne, pigmentation, anti-aging), general healthcare, dental and optometry. The Shopify shop sells supplements, serums, cleansers, collagen, moisturisers, pigmentation treatments, hair products and waist trainers.

**Brand:** primary green `#6a9e43` (darker `#5a8e33`, light tint `#eef5e7`), white, clean and clinical yet warm. Tone: professional and medical, but friendly, empathetic and aspirational — "real results from real practitioners." Never sleazy, never miracle-cure hype.

**Audience:** South African women 25–55 (plus a growing male segment), arriving from Facebook, Instagram and TikTok ads on their phones. They are results-driven but skeptical: they've been burned by products that didn't work. They trust before/after proof, medical credentials and word of mouth. **WhatsApp is their preferred channel** — every page must convert into a WhatsApp conversation or booking, not just a form submission.

## The one business goal

Convert cold ad traffic into **qualified leads within 60 seconds of landing**. A lead = name + WhatsApp number + their concern. Primary CTA everywhere: **book a free consultation via WhatsApp** (`https://wa.me/27727725449` with a pre-filled message containing the visitor's name, concern and preferred clinic). Secondary CTA: shop matched products.

## The 3 designs

**Design 1 — "Your Personal Treatment Plan" smart quiz funnel.**
A cinematic, full-screen branching quiz (skin / weight loss / aesthetics / wellness → 3–5 tailored questions each) with a progress bar, satisfying micro-animations on every answer, and a lead-capture gate before results. Results page: a personalised treatment plan card, matched products, testimonials, urgency element, WhatsApp booking CTA. Make answering feel addictive — like a BuzzFeed quiz with medical authority.

**Design 2 — "The Glow-Up Studio" interactive transformation experience.**
Built around proof: an interactive before/after slider gallery filterable by treatment (fillers, laser, weight loss, pigmentation), animated result stats ("9 kg down in 10 weeks", "dark marks faded in 6 weeks"), video-testimonial placeholders, and a floating "Get this result" button that opens a 3-field booking sheet (name, WhatsApp, treatment) ending in the WhatsApp CTA. The page should feel like scrolling a luxury aesthetic clinic's Instagram — but every element sells the consult.

**Design 3 — "Build My Package" interactive treatment calculator.**
Visitors tap treatments and goals to build their own glow-up package, watching a live summary panel update with what's included and an indicative value. Completing the build unlocks a limited-time consultation offer — but only after entering name + WhatsApp number. Include a payment-plan teaser, trust badges, and a sticky mobile CTA bar. This one kills price anxiety and captures the highest-intent leads.

## Non-negotiable conversion requirements (all 3 designs)

- Mobile-first: flawless at 375 px wide; thumb-reachable CTAs; sticky bottom CTA bar on mobile.
- Above the fold: a benefit-driven headline, one clear CTA, and a trust signal — within the first screen, no scrolling needed.
- Lead capture asks for the minimum: first name + WhatsApp number (email optional).
- Social proof throughout: 5-star testimonials with South African names, client counts, "registered professionals", both clinic locations.
- Urgency and scarcity used honestly (limited weekly consultation slots, this-week offer).
- POPIA-compliant privacy reassurance under every form.
- Micro-interactions everywhere: hover states, progress indicators, animated counters, smooth screen transitions. The pages must feel like a premium app, not a brochure.
- Load-light: no external frameworks; system fonts or one Google Font max; inline SVG icons.
- Every path ends at the WhatsApp CTA with a pre-filled message built from what the visitor entered.
